customers. The company primarily targets the maintenance, repair, and operations (MRO) sector, alongside automation

holding only a minor fraction of the total addressable market. Distributors act as the critical buffer between thousands of specialized component manufacturers and

millions of dispersed end-users. Scale is becoming the primary competitive moat, as only the largest distributors can afford the necessary

digital infrastructure and automated fulfillment centers.

sub-suppliers who struggle with labor shortages. Manufacturers are exerting pressure by extending payment terms up to 120 days, effectively forcing

distributors to fund their working capital requirements. Returns for top-tier distributors remain robust due to their ability to consolidate purchasing power and push
