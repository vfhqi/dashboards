Regulatory Paradigm Shift: DORA III Framework and Tariff Dynamics The regulatory landscape for Aena SME SA is undergoing a profound structural shift as the company transitions into the Third Airport Regulation Document (DORA III) for the 2027–2031 period. The expiration of this price ceiling has already materialized in the baseline tariffs leading into the new framework, with the National Markets and Competition Commission (CNMC) approving a 6.44% average tariff hike for 2026.
