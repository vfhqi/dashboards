Company Context

Margin Profile and Quality; KQ2: Commodity and Demand Risk

KQ2a: Commodity Hedge Position

  • 2026 protection: CEO confirmed "hedged against cost increases this year"; 2026 COGS profile largely locked via 12-18 month rolling hedges #J
  • 2027 exposure: No early 2027 hedging disclosed at Q1 2026; if aluminium/oil remain elevated through H2 2026, locking 2027 protection will cost materially more #J
